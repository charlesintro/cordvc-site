01 — what we do
we invest at the system layer—energy, matter, compute, and data—and in the expanding application layer that builds atop it.
the former is durable, capital intensive, and directional. the latter is fast-moving, divergent, and opportunistic.
cord vc was created to move across both with focus, fluency, and respect for the unique cadence each requires.

we believe capital is a creative act.
we believe ambition, when paired with clarity, can compound into things that matter.
we believe that capitalism has a role to play in building a better world.
this fund is an attempt to act on those beliefs—deliberately, precisely, and with taste.
